Our Vision: Shaping the future of biomedical imaging to improve health for all people

  • By developing cutting-edge imaging technology, we foster fundamental discovery and improve patient care
  • By training scientists & clinicians and disseminating our advancements, we drive the future of imaging in healthcare
  • Through a culture of collaboration, we bring people together to create new ways of seeing

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our shared infrastructure brings together medical and scientific personnel, creating ideal conditions for translational research. We operate multimodal human and animal imaging equipment exploring a range of MRI field and gradient strengths, providing advanced imaging capabilities for areas like microstructure MRI, PET/MRI, and MR-guided radiotherapy.

The core values behind our work

Community

We nurture a highly collaborative ecosystem, with rich collaborations spanning academia and industry, clinical and basic science, physics, biology, and computer science.

Bold Innovation

The contributions of our recognized leaders in imaging and translational science include pioneering work in rapid MRI, tissue microstructure, radiofrequency coil design, medical imaging AI grounded in rigorous physics, and clinical translation from head to toe.

Impact

We maintain a rare dual emphasis on medical imaging technology development and clinical translation, with a commitment to widely disseminating valuable and unique medical imaging research offerings, including open-source software tools and impactful datasets.

Researchers at the Center for Biomedical Imaging at NYU Langone Health develop transformative imaging technologies to advance basic science and address unsolved clinical problems.
